Game description

The purpose of this game is to complete goals which bring you points. In your aid will come upgrades that are applicable to your trains, such as speed doubling, teleportation from a station to another and doubling of points for that specific goal.

The game is turn based, which means that you also have a little bit of time to think about further tactical moves and to view some of the opponent's information, so that you consider all your options.

User Manual

At the start of the game each player will be given one goal to send a train from one location to another for a certain number of points. The longer the distance between the locations of a goal, the more points that will be awarded when the goal is completed. They can then choose a route for a train to travel from this starting location to the given end location. The aim of the game is to get more points than your opponent by completing goals using a combination of luck and tactics. Trains can be given better engines and upgrades (explained in the engines and upgrades section below) to increase their speeds and capabilities. At the start of each turn, players will be given a new goal until they have a maximum of 3, a new engine up to a maximum of 3 and an upgrade up to a maximum of 4. The player that reaches 1000 points first is the winner.
